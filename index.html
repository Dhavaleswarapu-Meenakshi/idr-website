About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated environments. Through collaboration with universities and industry organisations, IDR bridges academic insight with real-world cybersecurity, AI governance and technology risk practices.

Our Model

Academy

Training bootcamps and certification programs preparing students and professionals for careers in cyber and digital risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing AI governance frameworks, digital risk models and next-generation security technologies.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations in implementing global cybersecurity and risk frameworks such as NIST and ISO 27001.

Train → Hire → Innovate → Deploy

Community

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and practitioners across sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure. The institute builds a strong professional community focused on developing the next generation of digital risk specialists.
