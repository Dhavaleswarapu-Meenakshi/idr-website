The logo uses a geometric cube to symbolize structured digital systems, layered technology infrastructure, and resilience in managing digital risk. The orange top face represents innovation, emerging technologies, and forward-looking digital transformation, while the black and white sides convey security, stability, and trust. A clean modern sans-serif typeface (Inter) was chosen to create a professional technology and education identity that is easy to read across digital platforms. The minimalist design ensures the logo remains clear and recognizable at small sizes, such as favicons and mobile headers.
